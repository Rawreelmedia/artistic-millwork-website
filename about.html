Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1999

Artistic Millwork & Design was founded with a simple mission: to bring the timeless art of fine woodworking to modern homes and commercial spaces. What started as a small workshop has grown into South Florida's premier custom millwork provider.

For over 25 years, we've partnered with homeowners, architects, designers, and builders to create exceptional millwork that stands the test of time. Every piece that leaves our shop carries our commitment to quality, precision, and artistry.

Our team of master craftsmen combines traditional woodworking techniques with modern technology to deliver results that exceed expectations. We don't just build millwork—we create lasting impressions.
