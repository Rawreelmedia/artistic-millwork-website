FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A simple cabinet project might take 4-6 weeks, while a full kitchen or complex staircase could take 8-12 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

Do you offer installation services?

Yes! We provide full installation services with our own trained crews. This ensures the quality of our shop work carries through to the final installation.

What areas do you serve?

We serve all of Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach counties. For exceptional projects, we've also worked throughout Florida and can arrange shipping for cabinet and millwork components.

Can I visit your workshop?

Absolutely! We encourage clients to visit our shop to see our work firsthand, review material samples, and meet our team. Just schedule an appointment and we'll be happy to show you around.
